Meet Heather Kenagy

Bio

Born and raised in Oregon's lush Willamette Valley, Heather has built her life and career on the pursuit of wonderment.

When technology and the world wide web became widely available and accessible to the public in the mid-90s, Heather decided to pursue a career in tech. After graduating high school, Heather began a full-time tech career at Hewlett-Packard in Corvallis, Oregon. Seven years later, disillusioned by the corporate world's focus on shareholder profits, Heather sought employment in the non-profit sector and transitioned to Information Services at Samaritan Health Services in 2002. A regional healthcare system, Samaritan is comprised of 5 hospitals and 100+ outpatient clinics located in Linn, Benton and Lincoln Counties in Oregon.

As health systems transitioned to electronic health records (EHR) and Samaritan ultimately decided to adopt Epic Health System's EHR, Heather pursued a development role in building the EHR from the ground up. This pursuit required a commitment to certification in Epic EHR modules, of which Heather holds thirteen, along with 5 Epic badges. Ever-eager to expand her education and knowledge, Heather pursued a Master of Science in Health Informatics, graduating from University of New England in 2020.

As an Informaticist at Samaritan, Heather prides herself in being a results-focused, driven, self-directed employee with an excellent reputation for kindness, commitment and follow-through. Heather is also CompTia Project+ certified in project management, experienced in personnel and office management and is currently pursuing a professional certificate in JavaScript/CSS/HTML (MERN Full Stack) development. Heather believes in human-kindness and never tires of learning about the world around her. In her free time she enjoys being a collaborator, do-er, inspired student, pi memorizer (120 digits), musician, and lover of performing arts.
